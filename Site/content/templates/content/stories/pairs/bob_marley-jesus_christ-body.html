The images above are spiritual portraits, or Grojas.[1] They depict the personalities of Bob Marley, a central figure in Reggae music, and Jesus Christ, the central figure in the Christian religion.

xNFx: Blue and Red

Both images contain a lot of Blue and Red, because both Bob and Jesus tend to be idealistic and compassionate.

In terms of the four-letter types made popular by the MBTI®, both are xNFx — indicating both of them exhibit Carl Jung's iNtuition and Feeling personality traits.

Blue — iNtuition

The Blue in these images represents the trait Jung calls iNtuition. A person who is high in iNtuition tends to see things and events more in terms of ideas than facts.

Although Bob and Jesus were different in many ways, the idea of God plays a major role in both of their lives. Bob uses the term Jah rather than God in songs such as Forever Loving Jah[2] and Rastaman Vibration[2], but in my mind he and Jesus embrace the same idea.

As far as I'm concerned, anyone who asserts there is a significant difference between God and Jah is just being disagreeable — and unnecessarily defensive and divisive.

This idea that a higher power exists is one reason they are more idealistic than realistic. Now, this does not mean that everyone who is iNtuitive believes in Jah or God or Allah or the Seven Gods of Westeros, only that more pragmatic people tend to doubt the existance of something they cannot see, hear, touch, smell, or taste.

Red — Feeling

The Red in these images represents the trait Jung and the MBTI call Feeling. A person who is high in Feeling tends to be more emotional than logical, and tends to prefer working with other people rather than with inanimate things.

Both Bob and Jesus were compassionate and cared a lot about other people. While Jesus demonstrated his compassion by healing the sick, Bob makes his tendency in this respect clear in songs like Jammin',[2] in which he claims life is worth much more than gold.

Although Bob lacked Jesus' ability to perform miracles, he sought to comfort people with songs such as Three Little Birds,[2] in which he sings that every little thing gonna be alright and Johnny Was,[2] in which he seeks to comfort a woman whose son had been shot down in the street and died, just because of the system.

The positive emotions that Bob and Jesus warmth in these images

The emotions that Red represents in these images are not always positive, but in this case it represents the love that Bob and Jesus have and express for other people.

Differences

The images also show there are some differences between Bob and Jesus.

Most obviously the image of Bob's personality has more Blue, indicating he has more iNtuition. His image looks more open and accepting because he is more open-minded — more Perceptive — than Jesus.

Indeed, the P in ENFP stands for Perceiving — the opposite of Judging.

Also, the image of Jesus' personality has more Red, indicating he has more Feeling. His image looks warmer and heavier because he is more determined — more Judgmental — than Bob.

Indeed, the J in INFJ stands for Judging — the opposite of Perceiving.
